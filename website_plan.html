Focus, Shoot

Your cinematic movie discovery experience

Site Name

Focus, Shoot

This name represents the filmmaking process—focusing the camera and shooting a scene. It reflects the site's purpose of helping users discover, explore, and “capture” movies they love in a cinematic and engaging way.

Optional domain: focusshootmovies.com

Site Purpose

Focus, Shoot is a movie explorer website that allows users to search for films, discover trending and popular movies, and get personalized recommendations. Users can also sign up to receive notifications based on their favorite genres, actors, and movie preferences.

Scenarios

  • What movies are currently trending that match my favorite genres?
  • How can I sign up to receive notifications about new movie releases I would like?
  • Where can I find detailed information about a specific movie?

Color Scheme

  • Dark Blue (#0f172a) – Background color for the site
  • Cinematic Red (#e11d48) – Headers and important highlights
  • Gold (#facc15) – Accents, buttons, and emphasis

Typography

  • Poppins – Used for headings (modern, cinematic feel)
  • Roboto – Used for body text (clean and readable)
